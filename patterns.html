Patterns

Click to place at least 3 points. The algorithm with start in the middle and then follow these steps:

1. Randomly pick one of placed points
2. Move halfway between your current location and that point
3. Draw a dot at your location
4. Repeat.

With the right setup this can result in surprising patterns.
Try to guess what it will look like before hitting Start.
