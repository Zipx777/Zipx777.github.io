Game of Life

Activate squares by clicking on them, and then hit "Start" or "Step" to run through the simulation. Squares multiply or die off depending on a set of rules that can be customized below the board.

A living square dies unless it has between and living neighbors
A dormant square becomes alive if it has between and living neighbors
