"Ultimate TicTacToe"

The better version of TicTacToe, where you play on 9 TicTacToe squares at the same time. When one player makes a mark, the next player has to play in the square corresponding to that mark's location in its square. To win, a player must win 3 individual TicTacToe boards in a line.
