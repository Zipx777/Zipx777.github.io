Skill Descriptions

(names and functionality and art shamelessly stolen from WoW)

Core Skills

Q - Lightning Bolt

Moderate damage. Consumes up to 5 Maelstrom stacks to increase damage and reduce cast time by 20% per stack. Maelstrom stacks are generated as the player does melee damage, and maxes out at 10 stacks (shown as yellow dots on the player).

E - Storm Strike

High melee damage. Has a chance to have its cooldown reset each time you use a melee skill, including itself. 7.5-sec CD.

1 - Lava Lash

Moderate melee damage. 12-sec CD.

2 - Frost Shock

Low damage, shares a cooldown with other Shocks. 6-sec CD.

3 - Flame Shock

Very low initial damage. Applies an 18-second long DoT (shown as a shrinking red circle on the target). Shares a cooldown with other Shocks. 6-sec CD.

4 - Crash Lightning

Low melee damage. 9-sec CD.

DPS Cooldowns

Z - Bloodlust

40 seconds of increased attack speed and reduced melee skill cooldowns. 4-min CD.

X - Ascendance

For 15 seconds Storm Strike cooldown is drastically reduced, and damage and range increased on Storm Strike and auto-attacks. 2.5-min CD.

V - Feral Spirit

Generate 1 Maelstrom stack every 3 seconds for 15 seconds. 2-min CD.

R - Windfury Totem

Place a totem that lasts for 1 minute. When within range of the totem, auto-attacks have a chance to attack an extra time. For 12 seconds after placing the totem, melee attacks have a 100% chance to fire extra projectiles for bonus damage (without this buff, the chance is ~20%). 50-sec CD.

F - Sundering

High melee damage. 40-sec CD.

Utility Skills

C - Spirit Walk

15 seconds of 60% increased movement speed. 1-min CD.

5 - Ghost Wolf

Gain 30% movement speed. This cancels upon casting any skill (including itself again).
